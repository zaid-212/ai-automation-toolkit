# AI Automation Toolkit for Small Businesses

This project provides practical AI-powered tools and workflows to help small businesses automate marketing, customer communication, and website interactions.

## 🚀 Features

* AI chatbot response templates for websites
* SEO content generation workflows
* Lead capture and follow-up automation ideas
* FAQ generation for service businesses
* WordPress chatbot integration systems

## 🎯 Who is this for?

* Small business owners
* Freelancers
* Digital marketers
* WordPress users

## 💡 Use Cases

* Automate customer replies using AI
* Generate SEO-friendly content faster
* Improve website engagement with chatbot flows
* Save time on repetitive business tasks

## 🛠 Example

### Chatbot FAQ Template

Q: What services do you offer?
A: We provide customized solutions based on your needs. Contact us to learn more.

## 📈 Goal

To make AI accessible and useful for small businesses that cannot afford expensive tools.
